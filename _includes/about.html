How does one experience change our response to another? Synaptic priming fuses elements of synaptic plasticity with psychopharmacology to provide new answers to this question. As economically accessible, nomadic consultants, we use the synaptic priming framework to improve available treatment regimens, refine study designs, and develop novel therapeutics.

Theory

Synaptic priming integrates the foundations of metaplasticity with rapid-acting antidepressant pharmacology.

Results

Ketamine evokes long-lasting antidepressant actions by priming hippocampal plasticity.

Tools

Using the Plasticity Resolving Incubation Model Experimental (PRIME) design to elucidate time-sensitive antidepressant plasticity.
