What we do

Primer pharmacology

  • Designing novel antidepressant treatment paradigms that harness the unique pharmacology of ketamine and psychedelics
  • Combining available therapies to accelerate symptom relief, enhance therapeutic effects, and prolong symptom remission
  • Reducing patient and health-care provider burden by refining dosage regimens

Primer plasticity

  • Harnessing electrophysiology and behavioral tools to reveal druggable plasticity mechanisms
  • Advancing pharmacological, neuromodulatory, and other treatments for psychiatric disorders
  • Translating preclinical results to develop clinical bench-to-bedside projects

Collaborating to build a better future for psychiatric medicine

  • Providing hands-on training for diverse scientists
  • Bridging academic, government, and industry scientists in the Global North and South
  • Accessible price points tailored for developing international, emerging therapeutics
